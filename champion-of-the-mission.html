Champion of the Mission Session: Empowering Future Leaders for Service

The Champion of the Mission session at The Salvation Army Caribbean Territorial Training College is designed to inspire and equip cadets with a deeper understanding of the Salvation Army's mission. It challenges cadets to embrace their calling to serve and become passionate advocates for the mission as future officers.

The session emphasizes the importance of spreading the gospel and meeting human needs with selflessness, service, and compassion. Through teachings, activities, and discussions, cadets learn to embody the Salvation Armyâ€™s values and communicate its mission effectively.

The focus on leadership helps cadets develop qualities to inspire others and lead with integrity and compassion. The session also fosters collaboration and a sense of community, reinforcing the importance of teamwork in fulfilling the global mission. Ultimately, the Champion of the Mission session prepares cadets to lead with a deep commitment to serving others and advancing the Salvation Army's work.
