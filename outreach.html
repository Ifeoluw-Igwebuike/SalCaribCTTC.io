Empowering Cadets for Community Service and Ministry

Outreach in The Salvation Army is a fundamental part of cadet training, providing practical experience in serving the community and meeting both spiritual and physical needs. For cadets, outreach activities involve engaging with vulnerable groups, such as the homeless, at-risk youth, and individuals struggling with addiction. These initiatives allow cadets to develop essential skills in compassion, evangelism, and leadership. Through hands-on ministry, cadets learn to effectively communicate the gospel and offer support to those in need, preparing them for their future roles as officers in The Salvation Army.
